What is Jarvis & How AI Works

An AI voice assistant that helps with physics, general queries, and everyday tasks.

Uses NLP (spaCy) and OpenAI API to understand queries
Voice recognition and response
Step-by-step physics tutor and trigonometry solver
Can execute tasks like opening websites, telling time, etc.

Physics & Trigonometry Tutor

Recognizes numbers, units, and keywords from queries
Applies relevant formulas automatically
Step-by-step solution for physics questions
Trigonometry queries like sin30°, cos45° with exact values
Example snippet: “Calculate force: F = m × a”

AI Types

AGI – Artificial General Intelligence: Human-like problem solving
ASI – Artificial Super Intelligence: Beyond human intelligence (future concept)
